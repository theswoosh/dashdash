You aren't limited to one board. Create separate boards for different rooms,
purposes, or households, and switch between them from the config panel.

## Creating and switching boards

Open the config panel and use the board management section to create a new
board, rename one, or switch your active view. Each board has its own name
and its own set of widgets, laid out independently.

## What's shared, what's personal

The widgets on a board — which ones exist, where they're placed, and their
configuration — are shared by everyone who views that board. Move a
healthcheck tile in edit mode and it moves for every other user looking at
the same board too.

What stays personal to you is presentation, not content: your **theme**,
**wallpaper**, and **brightness** can each be overridden per board without
affecting anyone else viewing that board. Your account-level preferences
(language, whether the topbar is hidden, and similar display options) also
travel with you across boards.
